About

Being a team of one, I aim to form strong, interpersonal connections with my clients and understand the why behind what they want to achieve. That process begins by learning about their business and industry as I believe that good design is influenced by a strong understanding of these fundamentals.

Whether I'm designing a website, product, or pitch deck, I always aim to sweat the details, but know that done is sometimes better than perfect. I adapt my workflow and communication style to my clients needs on a case by case basis.
